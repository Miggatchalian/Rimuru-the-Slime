"That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime" tells the story of Satoru Mikami, a middle-aged office worker who is killed and reborn in a fantasy world as a slime, gaining unique abilities that allow him to absorb and mimic other creatures' skills; as a slime named Rimuru, he sets out to build his own nation of monsters, making allies with various powerful beings while navigating the political landscape of the new world, all while trying to live a peaceful life with his newfound friends.

Season 4 Announcement

The 4th season of the That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime anime. It was announced on September 27, 2024, following the conclusion of the 3rd season. In that same announcement, a 2nd movie was also announced.
